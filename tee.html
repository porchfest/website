Porchfest Shirts

Order your limited-edition West Philly Porchfest T-shirt!

If you pre-ordered a shirt before June 3rd (thank you!), you should have received an email confirmation with instructions on how to pick up your shirt: they will be available for pickup on June 8th, the Saturday of Porchfest, at the following locations: Clark Park Farmer’s Market (from 10am until noon), 934 S. Or, you can order a shirt right here, right now, over the internet.
