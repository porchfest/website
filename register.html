Registration

Porch registration will be open until 5 PM on June 3. Please register your show by Monday May 30 to have your porch listed on the printed version of the map.

Hosts

  1. Reach out to musicians and invite them to play on your porch during Porchfest. You can find musicians via the Musician/Porch Connector Facebook group. Be clear that all shows are free and uncompensated.
  2. Craft a schedule that fits within the suggested Start and End Times of your performance time zone.
  3. Register your porch by filling out this form.
  4. Pick up postcards to deliver on your block, tell people about your show and get ready for June 4!
