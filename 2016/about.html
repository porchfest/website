About

In 2007, the first Porchfest was held in Ithaca, NY. In the almost decade since then, 53 other Porchfests have sprung up in other US and Canadian cities such as San Francisco, Cleveland, Tucson, Montréal and Ottawa. 12 brand new Porchfests, including West Philly Porchfest, will be making their debut in 2016 alone!

West Philly Porchfest began when several folks who had participated in the Somerville, MA Porchfest enjoyed themselves so much that they decided to bring Porchfest back to West Philly. Plus, it seemed like the perfect fit for our neighborhood (we have better porches for sure!).

The first annual Porchfest took place on Saturday, June 4th with 75 acts playing shows at over 30 porches. This year's Porchfest will take place Saturday, June 3rd.

Performance times will be broken up by geographic location. Please refer to our interactive map on the main page for the most up-to-date porch and performer information.

Porch registration will be open until 5 PM on Friday June 2. If you want to have your porch listed on the printed map, you must register your porch no later than Monday May 29.
