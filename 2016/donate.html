Donate

West Philly Porchfest is an all-volunteer operation of dedicated local music lovers. We've kept our operating budget as low as possible. $400 in donations would cover all of the printing and other incidental costs from this year's festival – and it would be great to have even more than that to get a fund started for next year.

Make a donation via PayPal – we suggest an amount of at least ten dollars, but whatever you can give is helpful – and we'll send you a special commemorative edition of Kate Otte's gorgeous and delightful poster design (please specify if you would like a poster by adding a note to your payment).
